Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The BSIT program prepares students to become IT professionals who can design, develop, maintain, and manage information systems and IT infrastructures. The program equips learners with competencies in systems analysis, software development, database management, networks, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer / Programmer
  • Web & Mobile App Developer
  • Network Administrator
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Cybersecurity Analyst

Bachelor in Industrial Technology - Major in Computer Technology (BIT-CT)

The BIT-CT program focuses on the application of computing in industrial and technical environments. It strengthens hands-on skills in hardware servicing, networking, software tools, and applied troubleshooting.

Career Opportunities

  • Computer Technician
  • IT Service / Desktop Support
  • Network Cable Technician
  • Technical Drafting Aide
  • Computer Lab Custodian
  • Junior Network Administrator
  • AV / Multimedia Technician
  • IT Maintenance Staff
  • Technical Sales Support

Diploma in Mechatronics Technology* leading to BS Mechatronics

A specialized ladderized program integrating mechanical systems, electronics, robotics, and computer control systems. It prepares students for modern automation and manufacturing industries.

Career Opportunities

  • Mechatronics Technician
  • PLC Programmer Assistant
  • Robotics Technician
  • Industrial Maintenance Technician
  • Electro-Mechanical Technician
  • Automation Specialist
  • Instrumentation Technician

BTLED – Major in Information and Communication Technology

This program prepares future educators in ICT with strong foundations in digital literacy, computer systems, networking fundamentals, and ICT pedagogy for junior and senior high schools.

Career Opportunities

  • ICT Teacher (JHS / SHS)
  • Computer Lab Coordinator
  • Instructional Materials Developer
  • eLearning Support Staff
  • ICT Trainer
  • EdTech Specialist

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE)

The BSIE program focuses on optimizing systems, processes, and resources for productivity, efficiency, quality management, and data-driven decision making in industrial settings.

Career Opportunities

  • Industrial / Process Engineer
  • Supply Chain Analyst
  • Quality Assurance Engineer
  • Production Planner
  • Manufacturing Engineer
  • Safety & Efficiency Engineer
