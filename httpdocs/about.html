CTU Ginatilan Campus Enrollment System

The Cebu Technological University – Ginatilan Campus Enrollment System is a modern, secure, and student-centered platform designed to make the admission process simple and accessible at one of Cebu’s trusted state universities. This system allows applicants to register early and secure a slot for their chosen program, ensuring a smooth and organized start to their enrollment journey.

Updates Page

The system includes a dedicated Updates Page, where applicants can easily stay informed about official announcements, including:

  • Opening date of admissions
  • Schedule of entrance examinations
  • Interview dates and requirements

Academic Programs

Since 2018, CTU Ginatilan Campus has proudly offered:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor in Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology (BIT-CT)

Beginning School Year 2026–2027, three new programs will be added:

  • Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE)
  • Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education major in ICT (BTLED-ICT)
  • Diploma in Mechatronics Technology leading to BS Mechatronics

What Students Can Expect

  • Accessible, Affordable, and Quality Education – Supported by scholarships and government assistance.
  • Industry-Aligned, Competency-Based Learning – Hands-on training, labs, and practical experience.
