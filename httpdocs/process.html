Enrollment Process

1. Check Program Availability – View if there is still an available slot for the program you want.

2. Register an Account – Tap the LOG IN button, then in the lower part you can see a REGISTER button. Click it.

3. Complete the Form – Fill out the form then click Submit.

4. Wait for Admin Email – Wait for the Gmail response from the admin.

5. Follow Instructions – Follow the instructions sent by the admin and the required documents you must submit.

6. Submit Documents Face-to-Face – Wait for updates about when you can submit your requirements in person.

7. Final Enrollment Step – After submitting the requirements, wait for the update about the final step of enrollment.
