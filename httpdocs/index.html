Discover CTU Ginatilan through our 3-D campus model

Step into the future of learning with our fully rendered 3-D representation of CTU Ginatilan Campus. This interactive model allows you to explore the campus — from its modern multi-storey building to classroom layouts and campus grounds — giving you a realistic preview of student life and facilities even before you set foot on campus.

A campus built for growth and opportunity

Since its establishment in 2018, CTU Ginatilan has grown steadily, offering quality programs such as BS Information Technology and BS Industrial Technology (Computer Technology). In 2020–2021, a new 3-storey building with multiple classrooms was donated to the campus, reflecting the commitment of our partners and the community to expand and enhance learning facilities.

The 3-D model captures this milestone in vivid detail — showing a well-designed structure that blends functional classrooms with inspiring spaces for academic growth.

Envision yourself as part of our community

By navigating the 3-D model, you can imagine yourself attending classes, collaborating with classmates, and immersing in campus life. More than just a building, this campus stands as a symbol of ambition — a place where students from Southwest Cebu can access technology-rich education and forge pathways toward future careers.
